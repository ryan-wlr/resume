Registered Nurse Position
Memorial Healthcare System

We are seeking a compassionate and dedicated Registered Nurse to join our medical-surgical unit. The ideal candidate will provide high-quality patient care in a fast-paced hospital environment.

Responsibilities:
- Provide direct patient care including medication administration and monitoring
- Collaborate with healthcare team members including physicians and other nurses
- Document patient assessments and care plans in electronic health records
- Educate patients and families about health conditions and treatment plans
- Respond to medical emergencies and provide critical care when needed

Requirements:
- Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) preferred
- Active RN license in Florida
- BLS and ACLS certifications required
- Previous hospital experience preferred
- Strong communication and clinical skills
- Ability to work 12-hour shifts including nights and weekends

We offer competitive salary, comprehensive benefits, and opportunities for professional development in a supportive healthcare environment.
